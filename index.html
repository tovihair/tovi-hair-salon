About Tovi

TOVI has been in the hair industry for more than 30 years. During this time his skillset has seen him work in countries such as Korea, Thailand, Singapore and the UK.

During his earlier years, he participated in numerous overseas photo shoots. A particular highlight was his involvement in the “Super Elite Model Contest”. This gave him the opportunity to work with prestigious clientele such as Sora Lee, Jinkyung Hong, Jungeun Lee and Soyeon Kim.

He then decided to expand his career and came to the UK. Initially he was the Head Hairdresser at the hair salon “Agassi” before moving to be the creative director hair stylist at “The Retreat Hair and Beauty” Salon, which is an Aveda Hair salon. In between this time, he graduated with an “AA” graduate from the famous VIDAL Sassoon Academy on a postgraduate course.

He has now opened his own shop called Tovi Hair Salon on Park Road, New Malden.
